About

YOON SUNYOUNG

“I work to reveal to society what must be seen.”

I joined KBS in 2019 and have been working as a producer and television director. I mainly produce current affairs and documentary programs, including <In Depth of 60 minutes> and documentaries. From 2014 to 2018, I worked in the video editing team at SBS News. Drawing on that experience, I also published the essay comic <Yoon Jikwon's Peaceful World>, aimed at workers. I intend to continue documenting contemporary life through a variety of media.
